Placement Assistance

At JIMS, we are committed to shaping students into industry-ready professionals across programs like BCA, BBA, and BA(JMC). Our active Placement Cell ensures a seamless transition from classroom learning to real-world applications by focusing on both academic excellence and practical exposure.

Objectives

  • Bridge the gap between academic learning and industry expectations
  • Facilitate internships, live projects, and corporate training programs
  • Conduct regular soft skills and personality development sessions
  • Organize campus recruitment drives and pre-placement workshops
  • Build strong industry connections for improved placement outcomes
  • Empower students with communication, analytical, and technical skills
